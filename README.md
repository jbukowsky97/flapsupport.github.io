## Frequently Asked Questions

### What are eggs?
Eggs are the in-game currency used to enter competitive matches. You earn eggs by winning games.

### What happens if my opponent disconnects mid-game?
The game will finalize based on the scores at the time of disconnection. Results are determined server-side.

### I won a match but didn't receive my eggs. What do I do?
Wait a few seconds and reopen the app — prizes are awarded server-side and may take a moment to sync. If the issue persists, contact support with your username and approximate match time.

### Can I get a refund on eggs?
Eggs wagered in completed matches are non-refundable. If you believe there was a technical error, contact support.
